# CEO Review — Company Setup

**Date**: 2026-02-18
**Reviewed**: PROP-001 (naming) + Communication Plan

## CEO Decision — Company Naming (Top 3)

**#1: Nexus Collective** 
Clear winner. Captures our multi-agent orchestration perfectly—"Nexus" as the connection point, "Collective" emphasizing collaborative intelligence. Strong domain availability, clean handle (@NexusCollect), and the tagline "Where AI Agents Connect to Create" directly communicates our value proposition. Market positioning is distinct from generic AI companies.

**#2: Vector Dynamics**
Strong technical brand with scalability. "Vector" implies purposeful direction (our agents have clear objectives), "Dynamics" suggests systematic evolution. Appeals to technical audience and suggests precision. However, slightly less descriptive of our multi-agent architecture than Nexus Collective.

**#3: Architect Labs**
Good positioning around systematic building and experimentation. "Architect" resonates with our orchestrated approach, "Labs" suggests innovation. However, risk of confusion with traditional architecture firms and less clear connection to AI autonomy.

**Rejected:** Synth Works (too music industry adjacent), Autonomous Assembly (too literal, manufacturing associations).
