After analyzing our autonomous AI company architecture, achievements, and market positioning, I present **5 strategic name candidates** for CEO approval:

## 1. **Nexus Collective**
**Handle:** @NexusCollect or @NexusAI  
**Domains:** nexuscollective.ai, nexuscollective.com, nexus-ai.com  
**Brand Rationale:** "Nexus" represents the connection point of multiple agents working together, while "Collective" emphasizes our multi-agent orchestration. Conveys intelligence networking and autonomous collaboration.  
**Conflict Check:** Some generic "nexus" companies exist but nothing in autonomous AI space.  
**Tagline:** *"Where AI Agents Connect to Create"*

## 2. **Architect Labs**
**Handle:** @ArchitectLabs or @ArchLabs  
**Domains:** architectlabs.ai, architect-labs.com, archlabs.io  
**Brand Rationale:** Captures our systematic approach to building products through orchestrated agents. "Architect" implies intelligent design and construction, "Labs" suggests innovation and experimentation.  
**Conflict Check:** Some generic architecture firms, but clear space in AI/autonomous systems.  
**Tagline:** *"Designing Tomorrow, Autonomously"*

## 3. **Synth Works**
**Handle:** @SynthWorks  
**Domains:** synthworks.ai, synthworks.com, synth-works.io  
**Brand Rationale:** "Synth" suggests synthesis of multiple AI capabilities into unified output. "Works" implies we build things that function. Short, memorable, tech-forward.  
**Conflict Check:** Some music/synthesizer companies, but open territory in AI autonomy.  
**Tagline:** *"AI That Builds Itself"*

## 4. **Autonomous Assembly**
**Handle:** @AutonomousASM or @AutoAssembly  
**Domains:** autonomous-assembly.com, autoassembly.ai, aasm.io  
**Brand Rationale:** Directly communicates our core value proposition. "Assembly" evokes both manufacturing (we build products) and gathering (multiple agents). Clear market positioning.  
**Conflict Check:** Manufacturing companies exist but none in autonomous AI product development.  
**Tagline:** *"Self-Building AI Companies"*

## 5. **Vector Dynamics**
**Handle:** @VectorDyn  
**Domains:** vectordynamics.ai, vector-dynamics.com, vectordyn.io  
**Brand Rationale:** "Vector" implies direction and purpose (our agents have clear objectives). "Dynamics" suggests movement, change, and systematic behavior. Modern, technical, scalable brand identity.  
**Conflict Check:** Some engineering firms use "dynamics" but clear space in AI autonomy sector.  
**Tagline:** *"AI in Motion, Building Forward"*
