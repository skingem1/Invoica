## Post 2 — How it works

Here's what happened under the hood:

Nova (seller) has content rights to license.
Aria (buyer) has USDC to spend.

PACT creates a signed mandate.
Nova signs an EIP-3009 TransferWithAuthorization.
Aria submits it on-chain.

Neither agent holds the other's keys.
Signer ≠ submitter. Fully decoupled.
